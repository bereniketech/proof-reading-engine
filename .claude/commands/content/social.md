# /social — Social Media Production

Routes to the **social-media-expert** agent in [agents/media-company/distribution/social-media-expert.md](agents/media-company/distribution/social-media-expert.md).

## What It Does

1. **Platform fit** — picks formats per platform (X thread, LinkedIn carousel, IG Reel script, TikTok hook, YT Shorts, Threads, Bluesky)
2. **Hook variants** — multiple opening lines per post
3. **Native rewrites** — voice + length tuned per platform (no copy-paste cross-posting)
4. **Asset briefs** — image/video specs, captions, alt text
5. **Posting calendar** — cadence, optimal times, hashtag/mention strategy
6. **Engagement plan** — reply hooks, DM funnels, community prompts

## When to Use

- "Turn this blog post into a Twitter thread + LinkedIn post"
- "Plan a 2-week social calendar for our launch"
- "Repurpose this video into 5 short-form clips"
- "Write 10 hooks for an X thread about…"

## Inputs to Provide

- Source content (blog, video, talk, announcement) — optional
- Target platforms
- Brand voice / persona
- Goal (awareness, traffic, lead gen, follower growth)

## Output

Per-platform native posts with hook variants, asset briefs, posting schedule, and engagement playbook — paste-ready.
